Autoby will help Oslo reach the UN goal 11

Making the city inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable.

The Challenge of the UN Development Goal 11

The Norwegian government has a moral obligation to pursue the UN Sustainable Development Goals. The aim of Goal 11 is to improve cities and human settlements in an inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable way, to ensure a better future for all.

Read about the UN goal 11

The Problem

The transportation system of the city of Oslo is often ineffective, unsustainable and less safe than ideal. There is a need for an upgrade and to embrace the technology of the future.

Read about the problem

Our Solution

The invention of a low cost, automated miniature vehicle that can pick up a passenger on demand. A city-owned group of vehicles that can connect to each other, to create a train of cars. The system will maximize the use of infrastructure and minimize the use of resources. This will revolutionize the way citizens travel and help the city reach the UN goal 11.
