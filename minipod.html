The Minipod

The Minipod is an electric self-driving vehicle, meaning that it can take you from point A to B without you having to do much else than to know where you want to go. After setting a destination, at the press of a button, one of the Minipods will arrive at your location shortly and take you where you want to go.

A Solution for Everyone

The Minipod comes in many shapes and sizes! The standard Minipod is a two-person vehicle, however if you require a different type, you can change it to suit your needs. The other options include a 5 seater-pod, a wheelchair accessible-pod, and the shopping-pod with an extended trunk in addition or a car-trailer.

Perfect Connection

During rush hour, and whenever convenient, the Minipod will attach itself automatically to other pods. By implementing the locking mechanism on the MiniPod we can fully utilize the road capacity to reduce traffic congestion. Another effect of the perfect connection is that the air resistance is reduced, and by that, less energy is needed to move the pods.

How to get a Minipod?

There are several ways to get a Minipod! The easiest way is to order it is through the application, sett a destination, choose a type of pod, and it will be on its way! If you do not own a smartphone then use the MiniPod call centre, or visit any nearby kiosk or gas station, where you will get the help you need.

The MiniPods are stationed conveniently throughout the city and a Minipod is always around the corner. The waiting time for a pod is mere minutes!

Minipod App

The MiniPod app makes it easy to order a MiniPod to your current location. This interface could be included in the RuterReise-app for easy access to the MiniPod-System.

Functions of the App

  • Make a Profile
  • Choose the type of MiniPod
  • QR-code scanner to start a ride
  • A Map view while driving
  • Save trips for later use
  • Book a Minipod in advance

Great Power

Do you have range anxiety, fearing the MiniPod cannot take you the whole way?

Fear not, the pod will calculate the distance you need to go and how much battery it has left. If the battery is too low, the system will automatically assign you a new fully charged pod and send the low battery pod to an autonomous charging station.
