The Minipod

The Minipod is an electric autonomous self-driving vehicle, meaning that it can take you from point A to B without you having to do much else than to know where you want to go. After inputting where you want to go, at the press of a button, one of the Minipods will arrive at your location shortly and take you where you need to be.

A Solution for Everyone

The Minipod comes in many shapes and sizes! The standard Minipod is a two-person vehicle, however if you would like to use a different type, there are many ways to change it to suit your needs. Some of the other options include a family-pod with 5 seats, a wheelchair accessible-pod, and the shopping pod with an extended trunk in addition or a car-trailer.

How to get a Minipod?

There are several ways to get a Minipod! The main way to get one is to order it through the application, input where you want to go, if you have some extra needs, and a Minipod will be on its way! Other ways include calling a central switchboard where you will get the help you need. Visiting any nearby kiosk or gas station will also be possible, where the attendants will help you order one.

Great Power

Are you afraid the pod that comes to pick you up is out of battery, and can not take you the whole way?

Fear not, the pod will calculate the distance you need to go and how much battery it has left. If the battery is too low the system will automatically assign you a new fully charged pod and send the low battery pod to an autonomous charging station.
