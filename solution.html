Problem Solution The MiniPod

Our Solution

Our solution to the future transportation system of Oslo is the MiniPod. A city owned fleet of low cost, electric zero emission automated vehicles in different sizes that can handle all transportation needs within the city boundaries. A cost-efficient and effective transportation with flexible individual pods.

Sustainable trips

The MiniPod system will remove the need for private transportation within the city, giving a more sustainable way of travel. The ability of these pods to connect automatically to each other through electromagnets, will drastically increase the capacity and efficiency of existing infrastructure especially during rush hour. Saving the city form unnecessary emission and pollution.

The automated vehicles would eliminate the effects of human error in traffic. Making the city safer for both bicyclists and pedestrians, as well as drivers.

Accessibility

The MiniPod system is built with accessibility in mind, with the goal of making the city available to everyone, regardless of social status, disability or language proficiency. [Company Name] want to collaborate with the Norwegian government and make an even more inclusive public transportation system. Revolutionizing the way citizens travel and ensuring an inclusive, sustainable and safe city.
